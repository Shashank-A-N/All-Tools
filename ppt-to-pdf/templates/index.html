PPT ⇄ PDF Converter

Runs locally. No third-party APIs.

  • PPT → PDF: Needs LibreOffice or (Windows) PowerPoint+pywin32.
  • PDF → PPTX: Each PDF page becomes a slide image (looks identical; text not directly editable).
Tip: Install LibreOffice for cross-platform PPT → PDF. On Windows, PowerPoint gives best fidelity.
